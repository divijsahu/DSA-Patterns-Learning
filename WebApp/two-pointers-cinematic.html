Concept Architecture

Mental models for recognizing and solving

The Indicator

Sorted arrays, search spaces, or palindromic checks where pairs dictate the outcome. If you need O(1) space, think pointers.

The Invariant

Each move eliminates a section of the search space. Move left to increase sum. Move right to decrease sum.

The Guarantee

Pointers strictly move inward and never backtrack. Max operations equal the array length: exactly O(n).
